Steven DeVille

DeVille Interiors was established in 2003 by Steven Deville, following previous and extensive experience in furniture, restoration and kitchen manufacturing. Steven's philosophy is simple; to deliver beautiful, exacting craftsmanship that fulfils and exceeds the clients vision.

Steven makes and installs all his kitchens so you will receive a consistent point of contact and the personal attention your project requires. Steven will manage all the intricacies of each project using specialist and trusted tradesman when required, ensuring a seamless and perfect end result. The workshop is based in the heart of the Midlands a prime location to cover all areas of the UK.
